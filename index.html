About

Lead generation designed for businesses that want clarity.

Zorah is an Australian-owned lead generation company supplying verified leads to businesses across a wide range of industries. The model is straightforward: define the type of opportunities you want, receive aligned leads, and pay for the leads delivered.

For businesses that want a practical way to support growth without locking into heavy upfront marketing costs, Zorah offers a cleaner starting point.

How it works

Simple, transparent, and built to reduce wasted spend.
